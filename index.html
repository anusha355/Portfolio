Project 1: Plant Disease Detection Using Deep Learning Algorithsm

For my plant disease detection project, I used deep learning techniques to help identify plant diseases from images. I trained a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) on a large dataset of healthy and diseased plant leaves. This allowed the model to learn patterns in the images, such as specific spots or discolorations that are signs of disease. I then developed a mobile app that allows users to take a picture of a plant leaf, and the app instantly analyzes the image and predicts whether the plant is healthy or infected. The model is fast and accurate, helping farmers and gardeners identify issues quickly so they can take action to protect their crops..
